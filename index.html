Fifteen years old, he went to his first rave. Voyager 1, in the anchorage of the Brooklyn bridge, would completely change his life. This was in 1994. Techno & House would come to define who he is.

It was love at first sight. Lost in this new music he began attending New York City warehouse parties and evolving with the scene as he grew & matured. He began dabbling with writing Tech House immediately and has always had a project studio. Adam's influences are Eric Estornel (Maetrik & Maceo Plex), Maya Jane Coles, Richie Hawtin, Jamie Jones, and many more.

Adam left his NYC home, and has traveled across the States. Before leaving California, he promoted and DJed a weekly Tech House event in Napa Valley.

With a meticulous focus on quality and sound design, Adam prefers to create a unique sound he can call his own. Adam Shields is ever reaching for those next-level breakthroughs.
